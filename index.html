The Market Growth


Electric vehicles are the go-to alternative in the future. Skyrocketing prices of petrol and diesel and the concerns of long-term environmental issues have sparked a general trend towards all-electric vehicles. The number of EVs on our streets has significantly grown to almost about 5 million in 2019. With the likes of tesla mass producing electric autos and decreasing prices will make EVs an everyday sight on the roads.

Fatter Wallet

Electricity is more Affordable than gasoline.
More Efficient and Durable. Less Maintenance.

Electric motors are an incredibly efficient and durable technology, meaning less maintenance and a longer life time than gas engines.
BEV has fewer moving parts than a conventional petrol/diesel car. Servicing is relatively easy, less frequent and overall cheaper.

Cleaner & Greener

Made from Clean energy sources.
Less Emissions. Less Pollutions. Every time you drive an EV.

By choosing to drive an EV you are helping to reduce harmful air pollution from exhaust emissions. Reduced harmful exhaust emissions is good news for our health. Better air quality will lead to less health problems and costs caused by air pollution

Better Value

EV batteries are 90% recyclable.
Save more when you upgrade unlike gas engine cars.

EV batteries hold their charge for weeks at a time and are quick and easy to recharge. Many companies are looking at re-purposing used-EV batteries for second-life energy storage.
